Welcome to the Memory Game !


PLEASE READ the INSTRUCTIONS before YOU START


When you close this window, the Game will start.

Initially, all the cards will be open for 10secs. Try to memorize them. After 10secs cards will flip, and you'll have 2min to win the game. To win, you have to open all the cards again. Click a card to open it. Cards will stay open ONLY if you choose two cards with the same picture coherently. Otherwise, cards will flip back.


Good luck!
